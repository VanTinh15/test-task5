About us

The Englewood Torah Center (ETC) is dedicated to expanding Torah learning opportunities for men, women, and children

With over 70 active participants, we provide engaging classes, chavrutot, and community events to strengthen our connection to Torah.

Our Mission

ETC complements existing community learning by offering inclusive, flexible, and high-impact programming.

Founded to fill the need for additional structured learning, we aim to integrate Torah into daily life while fostering a sense of unity among all participants—regardless of shul affiliation.
